
# Video by Stream

Stream runs a full edge network of video SFU servers around the world

## What can you build

* Livestreaming experience with super low latency (50ms or so)
* Video calling
* Audio rooms
* Voice/Video AI

## Why use Stream

* Low latency voice & video. Edge network of servers around the world
* Great developer experience. Easy to integrate SDKs
* Dynascale. Automatically adjust the resolution and codec based on what's displayed on the screen, CPU, devices in the call and bandwidth
